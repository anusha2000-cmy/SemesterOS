Course: CS 257 - Database Systems
Semester: Fall 2026
Instructor: Prof. Daniel Kim
Credits: 3

Course Description:
This course covers relational database design, SQL, normalization, transactions, indexing, query optimization, and distributed databases. Students will complete homework assignments, labs, a team project, a midterm, and a final exam.

Grading Policy:
Homework: 25%
Labs: 15%
Midterm Exam: 20%
Team Project: 25%
Final Exam: 15%

Schedule and Deadlines:
Lab 1: SQL Basics - Due September 17, 2026
Project Milestone 1: Schema Design - Due October 8, 2026
Midterm Exam - October 19, 2026
Lab 2: Indexing and Query Plans - Due October 30, 2026
Project Milestone 2: Backend Integration - Due November 20, 2026
Final Project Presentation - December 7, 2026
Final Exam - December 16, 2026
