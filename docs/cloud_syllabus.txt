Course: CS 266 - Cloud Computing
Semester: Fall 2026
Instructor: Dr. Elena Martinez
Credits: 3

Course Description:
This course introduces cloud infrastructure, virtualization, containers, Kubernetes, serverless computing, monitoring, reliability, and cloud security. Students will complete labs, architecture assignments, a group project, and exams.

Grading Policy:
Labs: 30%
Architecture Assignments: 15%
Midterm Exam: 20%
Group Project: 25%
Participation: 10%

Assignments and Exams:
Lab 1: AWS EC2 and IAM Setup - Due September 12, 2026
Lab 2: Docker Containers - Due October 3, 2026
Group Project Proposal - Due October 7, 2026
Midterm Exam - October 23, 2026
Lab 3: Kubernetes Deployment - Due November 3, 2026
Group Project Demo - November 30, 2026
Group Project Report - Due December 5, 2026
