Course: CS 271 - Machine Learning
Semester: Fall 2026
Instructor: Dr. Maya Rao
Credits: 3

Course Description:
This course introduces supervised learning, unsupervised learning, neural networks, model evaluation, and responsible AI. Students will complete programming assignments, quizzes, a midterm, a final project, and a final exam.

Grading Policy:
Programming Assignments: 30%
Quizzes: 10%
Midterm Exam: 20%
Final Project: 25%
Final Exam: 15%

Important Dates:
Quiz 1: Probability and Linear Algebra Review - September 23, 2026
Project Proposal - Due October 2, 2026
Midterm Exam - October 21, 2026
Project Checkpoint - Due November 18, 2026
Final Project Report and Demo - Due December 4, 2026
Final Exam - December 14, 2026

Week 1: Introduction to Machine Learning
